Easy Reader - Chrome Extension

About Easy Reader

Easy Reader is a powerful Chrome extension that enhances readability and provides a comfortable reading experience. It offers features like Dark Mode and Text Size Adjustment, allowing you to customize the appearance of web pages according to your preferences.

Dark Mode

Easy Reader comes with a built-in dark mode that enhances readability in low-light environments. Activate dark mode to reduce eye strain and enjoy a more comfortable reading experience.

Text Size Adjustment

With Easy Reader, you can easily adjust the text size to suit your preferences. Increase or decrease the font size to enhance readability and make content more accessible for all users.
